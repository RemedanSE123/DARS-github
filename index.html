Address Registration

Easily register and manage addresses at all levels, ensuring complete and precise address information:
Comprehensive Address Registration
Easily register and manage addresses from the region, zone, district (woreda), sub-district (kebele), neighborhood (mender), to the house number.
  • Region: Start by selecting the region.
  • Zone: Drill down into zones within the region.
  • District (Woreda): Further specify by choosing the district.
  • Sub-District (Kebele): Narrow it down to the sub-district.
  • Neighborhood (Mender): Identify the specific neighborhood.
  • House Number: Finally, enter the exact house number.

Contribution Guidelines

    Accurate information
    • Provide proof of the address, such as a map or other supporting documentation.
    • We do not recommend adding region and zone information. Instead, focus on providing the woreda, kebele, mender, and house number.
    • Every piece of information you add will be checked by the system administrator for accuracy.

    Report Issues

    • Report Issues: Found a bug or an error? Report it through our feedback system.
    • Suggest Features: Have an idea for a new feature? Let us know!
    • Share Your Knowledge: Write blogs about your experiences, travel stories, or cultural insights.
    • Spread the Word: Share DARS with your friends, family, and community to help others benefit from our system.
    • Collaborate: If you're a developer or designer, join our team to enhance DARS further.

  • 3

    Join Us

    Be a part of the digital transformation journey in Ethiopia. With DARS, we aim to make address registration, navigation, and information sharing easier and more accessible for everyone. Join us in building a smarter, more connected Ethiopia.

    Motivation

    The motivation behind DARS is to simplify and digitalize address management in Ethiopia. By providing a comprehensive and interactive platform, we aim to enhance navigation, improve logistics, and boost tourism. DARS bridges the gap between traditional address systems and modern digital solutions, ensuring everyone can easily find and register addresses.
